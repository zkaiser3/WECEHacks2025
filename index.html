Welcome to WECE Hacks

What's WECE Hacks?

Join us in February 2025 for WECE Hacks, a hackathon hosted by Women in Electrical & Computer Engineering at UIUC. Work in teams to create a resume-worthy project combining hardware and software. Face cybersecurity challenges, meet new students, and network with our sponsors! No experience is needed; this is a great opportunity to create your first coding project. For more information, fill out the interest form below.

ALL majors and genders are welcome!
